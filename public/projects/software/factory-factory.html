Factory Factory
2023

An interactive AI generated TV show

Factory Factory was a TV show about a chaotic, interactive, AI generated factory! It took requests from Twitch chat to generate new content in realtime using ChatGPT and Unity.

The project was designed to look like low resolution 3D simulation software used in a factory setting. I made most of the factory models myself in Blender.

Factory Factory streamed live on Twitch. It has now shutdown, but the source is available on GitHub.
