Vega (Nickelodeon All-Star Brawl)
2023

A complex model swap for Nickelodeon All-Star Brawl!

This project required a significant amount of research and trial and error. Nickelodeon All-Star Brawl (NASB) does not support model swaps, and Vega is swapped onto a character with the most complex rig in the game, Jenny Wakeman (XJ-9).

Jenny Wakeman (XJ-9) is from the TV show My Life as a Teenage Robot. Vega is sort of a parallel reality version of Jenny that lives on a different planet. Her design is similar, but more bug-like!

My custom Vega model (left) vs Jenny's official model (right). Jenny vs Jenny!

The biggest challenge was figuring out how to get the model to import correctly into the game. The order of the bones must match what the game is expecting. Blender exports bones in alphabetical order, but the game's models weren't made in Blender!

I had to write a C# script to rearrange the bones on import, and another to show the bone order in the inspector to verify it was working.

Finally, I used Steven's Alt Skins Rehydrated template Unity project and skin export script to create the Unity AssetBundle that the mod loads into the game!
