Graffiti Walls
2022

Graffiti Walls is a community driven VRChat world that provides the best spray-painting experience in VRChat!

This project was a collaboration with my friends HyperVirtualExtreme and Ivanmz! I did Udon programming, 3D modeling, sound design, and project management.

We would sometimes host a "synced instance" where it was possible to see the artwork that has been painted before you joined the world!
