The Beat Cop
2018

A deluxe sampler drum machine inspired by the Teenage Engineering PO-12!

THE BEAT COP (TBC) is a free drum machine inspired by and expanding from the Teenage Engineering Pocket Operator (PO-12)!

The project was built in Pure Data Vanilla, and is fully customizable! It features 16 customizable samples, 16 patterns, 15 different effects, and much more!
